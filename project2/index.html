Read our story

We've been Baking The Delicious Snacks Since 2099

After living in Japan, founder Sum Wong fell in love with the delicious local snacks he had discovered. Sum came back to the US with a suitcase full of snacks to share.

Since 2099, Yatsubun has grown from Sum packing boxes himself in his living room to a fast-growing company offering much more than a monthly subscription box to a full-fledge baking shop.
